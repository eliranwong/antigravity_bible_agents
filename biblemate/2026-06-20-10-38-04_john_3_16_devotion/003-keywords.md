### Key Word Analysis for John 3:16

1. **ἠγάπησεν (agapaō - G25)**: Divine, sacrificial love based on choice and character rather than mere emotion. It is active and gives.
2. **μονογενῆ (monogenēs - G3439)**: Unique, only-begotten, or one-of-a-kind. Highlights the unique relationship between the Father and the Son.
3. **πιστεύων (pisteuō - G4100)**: Trusting, believing, placing faith in. Not merely intellectual assent but personal reliance on Christ.
4. **ζωὴν αἰώνιον (zōē aiōnios - G2222, G166)**: Eternal, everlasting life. Life of the age to come, starting now and continuing forever in fellowship with God.
