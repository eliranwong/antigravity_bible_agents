# BibleMate Study: Beyond the Beginning - Joshua 3 Sermon

## Original User Request
Perform a deep exegetical and theological analysis, and then write a comprehensive expository sermon on Joshua 3 'Beyond the Beginning' with discussion questions, following Billy Graham preaching style and Bill Johnson theology.

## Refined User Request
Perform a deep historical-grammatical and theological study of Joshua 3. Based on this study, write a comprehensive, publication-quality expository sermon titled "Beyond the Beginning" that builds through three distinct, non-repetitive, actionable points. The sermon should address contemporary worldviews, address real struggles of mature Christians and young adults, and include detailed historical backgrounds, illustrations, applications, and a closing prayer. The theological framework should incorporate themes of hosting the Presence of God and faith-risk (inspired by Bill Johnson), and the preaching style should carry the evangelistic clarity, biblical authority, and salvation call of Billy Graham. Include three small group discussion questions.
