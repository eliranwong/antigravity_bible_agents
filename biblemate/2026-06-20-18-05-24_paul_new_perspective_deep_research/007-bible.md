**Galatians 3:1**
- **[ESV2016]** O foolish Galatians! Who has bewitched you? It was before your eyes that Jesus Christ was publicly portrayed as crucified.
- **[NASB2020]** You foolish Galatians, who has bewitched you, before whose eyes Jesus Christ was publicly portrayed as crucified?

**Galatians 3:2**
- **[ESV2016]** Let me ask you only this: Did you receive the Spirit by works of the law or by hearing with faith?
- **[NASB2020]** This is the only thing I want to find out from you: did you receive the Spirit by works of the Law, or by hearing with faith?

**Galatians 3:3**
- **[ESV2016]** Are you so foolish? Having begun by the Spirit, are you now being perfected by the flesh?
- **[NASB2020]** Are you so foolish? Having begun by the Spirit, are you now being perfected by the flesh?

**Galatians 3:4**
- **[ESV2016]** Did you suffer so many things in vain—if indeed it was in vain?
- **[NASB2020]** Did you suffer so many things in vain—if indeed it was in vain?

**Galatians 3:5**
- **[ESV2016]** Does he who supplies the Spirit to you and works miracles among you do so by works of the law, or by hearing with faith—
- **[NASB2020]** So then, does He who provides you with the Spirit and works miracles among you, do it by works of the Law, or by hearing with faith?

**Galatians 3:6**
- **[ESV2016]** just as Abraham “believed God, and it was counted to him as righteousness”?
- **[NASB2020]** Just as Abraham BELIEVED GOD, AND IT WAS CREDITED TO HIM AS RIGHTEOUSNESS.

**Galatians 3:7**
- **[ESV2016]** Know then that it is those of faith who are the sons of Abraham.
- **[NASB2020]** Therefore, recognize that it is those who are of faith who are sons of Abraham.

**Galatians 3:8**
- **[ESV2016]** And the Scripture, foreseeing that God would justify the Gentiles by faith, preached the gospel beforehand to Abraham, saying, “In you shall all the nations be blessed.”
- **[NASB2020]** The Scripture, foreseeing that God would justify the Gentiles by faith, preached the gospel beforehand to Abraham, saying, “ALL THE NATIONS WILL BE BLESSED IN YOU.”

**Galatians 3:9**
- **[ESV2016]** So then, those who are of faith are blessed along with Abraham, the man of faith.
- **[NASB2020]** So then, those who are of faith are blessed with Abraham, the believer.

**Galatians 3:10**
- **[ESV2016]** For all who rely on works of the law are under a curse; for it is written, “Cursed be everyone who does not abide by all things written in the Book of the Law, and do them.”
- **[NASB2020]** For all who are of works of the Law are under a curse; for it is written: “CURSED IS EVERYONE WHO DOES NOT ABIDE BY ALL THE THINGS WRITTEN IN THE BOOK OF THE LAW, TO DO THEM.”

**Galatians 3:11**
- **[ESV2016]** Now it is evident that no one is justified before God by the law, for “The righteous shall live by faith.”
- **[NASB2020]** Now, that no one is justified by the Law before God is evident; for, “THE RIGHTEOUS ONE WILL LIVE BY FAITH.”

**Galatians 3:12**
- **[ESV2016]** But the law is not of faith, rather “The one who does them shall live by them.”
- **[NASB2020]** However, the Law is not of faith; on the contrary, “THE PERSON WHO PERFORMS THEM WILL LIVE BY THEM.”

**Galatians 3:13**
- **[ESV2016]** Christ redeemed us from the curse of the law by becoming a curse for us—for it is written, “Cursed is everyone who is hanged on a tree”—
- **[NASB2020]** Christ redeemed us from the curse of the Law, having become a curse for us—for it is written: “CURSED IS EVERYONE WHO HANGS ON A TREE”—

**Galatians 3:14**
- **[ESV2016]** so that in Christ Jesus the blessing of Abraham might come to the Gentiles, so that we might receive the promised Spirit through faith.
- **[NASB2020]** in order that in Christ Jesus the blessing of Abraham would come to the Gentiles, so that we would receive the promise of the Spirit through faith.
