## Refined User Request
A comprehensive, publication-quality theological and exegetical study examining the New Perspective on Paul (NPP). The study will trace the historical development of the NPP (initiated by E.P. Sanders, James D.G. Dunn, and N.T. Wright), examine key Pauline texts, analyze Greek terminology (erga nomou, dikaiosyne theou, pistis Christou), and compare the traditional Protestant/Reformed view of justification by faith alone with the NPP's covenantal/ecclesiological formulation, concluding with balanced theological critique, pastoral applications, and prayers.
