**Romans 9:30**
- **[ESV2016]** What shall we say, then? That Gentiles who did not pursue righteousness have attained it, that is, a righteousness that is by faith;
- **[NASB2020]** What shall we say then? That Gentiles, who did not pursue righteousness, attained righteousness, but the righteousness that is by faith;

**Romans 9:31**
- **[ESV2016]** but that Israel who pursued a law that would lead to righteousness did not succeed in reaching that law.
- **[NASB2020]** however, Israel, pursuing a law of righteousness, did not arrive at that law.

**Romans 9:32**
- **[ESV2016]** Why? Because they did not pursue it by faith, but as if it were based on works. They have stumbled over the stumbling stone,
- **[NASB2020]** Why? Because they did not pursue it by faith, but as though they could by works. They stumbled over the stumbling stone,

**Romans 9:33**
- **[ESV2016]** as it is written, “Behold, I am laying in Zion a stone of stumbling, and a rock of offense; and whoever believes in him will not be put to shame.”
- **[NASB2020]** just as it is written: “BEHOLD, I AM LAYING IN ZION A STONE OF STUMBLING AND A ROCK OF OFFENSE, AND THE ONE WHO BELIEVES IN HIM WILL NOT BE PUT TO SHAME.”

**Romans 10:1**
- **[ESV2016]** Brothers, my heart’s desire and prayer to God for them is that they may be saved.
- **[NASB2020]** Brothers and sisters, my heart’s desire and my prayer to God for them is for their salvation.

**Romans 10:2**
- **[ESV2016]** For I bear them witness that they have a zeal for God, but not according to knowledge.
- **[NASB2020]** For I testify about them that they have a zeal for God, but not in accordance with knowledge.

**Romans 10:3**
- **[ESV2016]** For, being ignorant of the righteousness of God, and seeking to establish their own, they did not submit to God’s righteousness.
- **[NASB2020]** For not knowing about God’s righteousness and seeking to establish their own, they did not subject themselves to the righteousness of God.

**Romans 10:4**
- **[ESV2016]** For Christ is the end of the law for righteousness to everyone who believes.
- **[NASB2020]** For Christ is the end of the Law for righteousness to everyone who believes.

**Romans 10:5**
- **[ESV2016]** For Moses writes about the righteousness that is based on the law, that the person who does the commandments shall live by them.
- **[NASB2020]** For Moses writes of the righteousness that is based on the Law, that the person who performs them will live by them.

**Romans 10:6**
- **[ESV2016]** But the righteousness based on faith says, “Do not say in your heart, ‘Who will ascend into heaven?’” (that is, to bring Christ down)
- **[NASB2020]** But the righteousness based on faith speaks as follows: “DO NOT SAY IN YOUR HEART, ‘WHO WILL GO UP INTO HEAVEN?’ (that is, to bring Christ down),

**Romans 10:7**
- **[ESV2016]** “or ‘Who will descend into the abyss?’” (that is, to bring Christ up from the dead).
- **[NASB2020]** or ‘Who will descend into the abyss?’ (that is, to bring Christ up from the dead).”

**Romans 10:8**
- **[ESV2016]** But what does it say? “The word is near you, in your mouth and in your heart” (that is, the word of faith that we proclaim);
- **[NASB2020]** But what does it say? “THE WORD IS NEAR YOU, IN YOUR MOUTH AND IN YOUR HEART”—that is, the word of faith which we are preaching,

**Romans 10:9**
- **[ESV2016]** because, if you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.
- **[NASB2020]** that if you confess with your mouth Jesus as Lord, and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you will be saved;

**Romans 10:10**
- **[ESV2016]** For with the heart one believes and is justified, and with the mouth one confesses and is saved.
- **[NASB2020]** for with the heart a person believes, resulting in righteousness, and with the mouth he confesses, resulting in salvation.

**Romans 10:11**
- **[ESV2016]** For the Scripture says, “Everyone who believes in him will not be put to shame.”
- **[NASB2020]** For the Scripture says, “WHOEVER BELIEVES IN HIM WILL NOT BE PUT TO SHAME.”

**Romans 10:12**
- **[ESV2016]** For there is no distinction between Jew and Greek; for the same Lord is Lord of all, bestowing his riches on all who call on him.
- **[NASB2020]** For there is no distinction between Jew and Greek; for the same Lord is Lord of all, abounding in riches for all who call on Him;

**Romans 10:13**
- **[ESV2016]** For “everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved.”
- **[NASB2020]** for “EVERYONE WHO CALLS ON THE NAME OF THE LORD WILL BE SAVED.”
