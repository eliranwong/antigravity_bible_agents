# Trusting the Lord in All Our Ways: A Study of Proverbs 3:5-6

**Scripture Reference (NET)**:
> **Proverbs 3:5-6**
> [5] Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not rely on your own understanding. [6] Acknowledge him in all your ways, and he will make your paths straight.

## Reflection

My friends, the Bible tells us in Proverbs to place our absolute, undivided trust in the Lord. When we look at the world around us, we see people running in every direction, trying to find their own way, relying on their own human wisdom and clever schemes. But the Word of God calls us to a different path—a path of total surrender and faith.

What does it mean to trust the Lord with all our heart? It means holding nothing back. It means laying down our doubts, our fears, our intellectual pride, and our self-reliance at the foot of the cross. We must look to Jesus, who died for our sins and rose again to give us eternal life. He is the Way, the Truth, and the Life.

When we acknowledge Him in all our ways—in our decisions, our daily walks, our relationships, and our work—He promises to make our paths straight. He will guide our steps, clear the obstacles, and lead us home. Today, let us turn away from our own understanding, repent of our self-will, and put our hand in the hand of the Savior.

## Prayer

Heavenly Father, we come before You today acknowledging that our own wisdom is nothing compared to Your infinite truth. We confess that too often we have leaned on our own understanding. Right now, we surrender our lives, our plans, and our hearts to You. Teach us to trust You fully. Guide our paths, and let Your Holy Spirit direct our steps in righteousness. In the precious name of Jesus, Amen.
